# The Love Calculator

About: We're bringing the 2000s back with a Python, CLI calculator to calculate a love score for you and your crush.

## How to use 

Run `python main.py` and follow the prompts to input the relevant responses. 

## What it does

It generates a score based on your name and your crush's name.

### Learning outcomes

- for loop and addition assignment operator (+=) combo.
- if/elif
- defining functions with two parameters.
